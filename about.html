Our Story

Medidorm Limited was founded by Stephanie Nwaigwe, an experienced Advanced Clinical Practitioner trainee with extensive experience in healthcare leadership and domiciliary care. The company bridges the gap between social care and modern community health — providing holistic, safe, and person-centred support.
