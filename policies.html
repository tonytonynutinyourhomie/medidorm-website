Policies & Governance

Medidorm operates under robust professional governance and internal quality frameworks.

Clinical oversight applied where required; services reviewed regularly with governance processes in place.

Information Governance & GDPR Compliance

We handle personal data in line with GDPR and good information governance practices.

Clear safeguarding procedures and an accessible whistleblowing policy are in place to protect clients and staff.

Risk assessments and lone working policies keep staff and clients safe.
