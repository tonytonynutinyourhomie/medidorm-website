Our Services

We provide a broad range of non-regulated support services designed to promote independence and wellbeing.

Home Support & Lifestyle Services

  • Domestic support (cleaning, laundry, meal prep, errands)
  • Shopping and household organisation
  • Light mobility assistance and escorting to appointments
  • Companionship, conversation, and emotional support
  • Digital and administrative help — online forms, letters, medication reminders

Community Wellbeing & Preventive Health

  • Blood pressure, weight, and wellbeing monitoring visits
  • Nutritional and exercise motivation support
  • Coaching for sleep hygiene, stress reduction, and self-care
  • Medication reminders and adherence support
  • Coordination with GPs or community teams (with consent)

Family & Carer Support

  • Respite and sitting services for carers
  • Care coordination and information guidance
  • Support after hospital discharge or recovery
  • Home safety and wellbeing checks

Future Clinical Services (Planned for Launch)

Once independent prescribing qualification is achieved, Medidorm plans to introduce a Private Prescribing and Lifestyle Health Service including:

  • Private weight management programmes (e.g. GLP-1 therapy)
  • Smoking cessation support
  • Minor illness management (e.g. respiratory, skin, gastrointestinal)
  • Hormone and women’s health wellbeing
  • Medication reviews and deprescribing support

All prescribing services will comply with UK professional and legal frameworks under NMC and MHRA regulations.
